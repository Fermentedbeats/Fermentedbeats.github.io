DBC Value Exploration


Date: October 8, 2014

This week's post touched on DBC's values exploration.

When I think back on the times where I've been happiest, proudest, or satisfied the values involved are usually among: Adventure, Accomplishment, Ambition, Competition, Creativity, Enthusiasm, Excitement, Human-Connection, Inspiration, Mentorship, Money, Optimism, or Personal Development. At any given time I've got these values cranked up to 11. Spinal Tap reference

From ths list the two that recur most are:

  • Competition brings out the best in me. Competition helps people explore limitations, and gives motivation to improve.
  • Creativity I find the greatest satisfaction in producing something tangible, hence why software development presents itself as such a rewarding career.

Other notes:
  • The last topic I was asked advice for was on money management. Maintaining self-sufficiency in regard to finances has always been a top value.
  • In regard to last week's post on Stereotype Threat; I'm hedging my bets with ambition, accomplishment, and competition. Tell me I can't do something and I absolutely will.
  • Getting through the hard times at DBC will fall back to ambition and optimism.
What are your strongest values? Send me an email and tell me about it.
